The internet hotline is an initiative of the Film and Publication Board. The Film and
Publication Board was established by parliament to regulate distribution of films and
publication with the aim of protecting children from exposure to illegal, harmful and inappropriate material.

The Primary Purpose of the FPB Hotline is to prevent distribution of child sexual abuse material
when detected online or on any other media

The FPB Hotline provides a secure and confidential environment to members of the public when reporting
child sexual abuse material. The hotline staff adheres to the code of ethics(which outs emphasis on confidentiality)
as prescribe by National and International Bodies such as the International Associations of Internet Hotline.


The Content Analysts who make up the hotline team will assess the contents of the alleged
child sexual abuse material, and take appropriate action, where the content is confirmed to contain
child sexual abuse material, by reporting it to law enforcemet agencies and instututing a take down.



The internet hotlinewill also foward a detailed report relating to child ponography to the law enforcement agencies with the country for prosecution.Our International networking and imminent partneship with INHOPE allows us to take action agains child pornography(child sexual abuse images) on the internet hosted outside South Africa.

These international networks will then pass our
reports to the appropriete law enforcement agencies.



The website is also intended to alert Internet Service Prodivers of the criminal Activities , relateing to child pornography and or sexual abuse images used/ hosted on their servers or distributed through their Infrastructure

The hotline will be available 24hrs a day and 7 days a week to enable members of the public to Immediately report discovered child ponography (child sexual abuse images). All reports will be Acknowledge . Processed and Appropriate Feedback will be Provided
