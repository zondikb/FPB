COMMUNITY OUTREACH


The internet hotline is an initiative of the Film and Public Board. The Film and
Publication Board was established by parliament to regulate distribution of films and
publication with the aim of protecting children from exposure to Inappropriate material.
The internet hotline is a service that affords the members of the publics with an oppotunity
to report , online , any child ponography or sexual abuse images discoved accidentally
on the internet. This may also includes child grooming activities hosted in the chat rooms.
