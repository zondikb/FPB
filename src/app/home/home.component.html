Creating a Safer Environment
Online and Offline


The internet hotline is an initiative of the Film and Publication Board.
The FPB was established by parliament to regulate distribution of films and publications with the aim of protecting
children from exposure to illegal, harmful and inappropriate material.


The internet hotline is a service that affords the members of the public with an oppotunity to anonymous report,
online, any child pornography or sexual abuse and exploitation images discovered on the internet.
